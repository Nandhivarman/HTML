CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

PREP TIMES:15 MINUTES

COOK TIMES:10 MINUTES

Steps
  1. product the oven to 3500F(1800C)
  2. mix flow and sugar in a bowel
  3. add chocolate chips and oil well
  4. baks for 10 minutes or until golden brown
Ingrediants
 
Ingrediant Quantity
flow 2 cups
sugar 1 cups
chocolate chips 2 cups
